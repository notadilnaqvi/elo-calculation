The DNN works best for values between Elo range 2000-2700 with approximate 100 Elo point difference.

A deep neural network (DNN) works behind the scenes to predict the updated Elo. The equation that this DNN approximates i.e. the actual Elo formula used by the International Chess Federation (FIDE) is:
\[\Delta Elo = k * \{result - {1 \over {1+10^{oppElo - yourElo \over 400}}}\}.\]
